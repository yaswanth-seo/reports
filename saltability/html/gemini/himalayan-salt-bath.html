* **Skin Hydration:** Unlike regular salt which can be drying, Himalayan salt helps the skin retain moisture and can soothe irritation or redness. * **Relaxation:** The warm water combined with magnesium helps ease muscle tension and promotes better sleep. * **Exfoliation:** It naturally sloughs off dead skin cells, leaving you feeling much smoother. * **Mineral Absorption:** Proponents suggest that your skin absorbs trace minerals through "dermal absorption," though the science on the extent of this is still debated.
