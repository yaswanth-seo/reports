A **Salt Stone Massage** is a therapeutic treatment that uses warm stones carved from Himalayan salt—rather than the traditional basalt river stones—to soothe muscles and nourish the skin.
It is essentially a "mineral-rich" twist on the classic hot stone massage. Here is a breakdown of what makes it unique:
### Key Features and Benefits
* **Deep Relaxation:** The heat from the stones helps to penetrate deep into muscle tissue, allowing the therapist to release tension without using excessive pressure. * **Natural Exfoliation:** As the salt stones glide over the skin, they act as a very gentle exfoliant, leaving your skin feeling smoother and more hydrated. * **Mineral Absorption:** Himalayan salt contains 84 trace minerals (including magnesium, calcium, and potassium). ### What to Expect During a Session
The therapist will heat the stones (usually to about **120°F–130°F**) and use them as an extension of their hands. They will apply a light oil to the skin to prevent the salt from being too abrasive and to allow the stones to glide easily. You’ll feel a combination of rhythmic strokes and stationary stone placement on specific trigger points.
