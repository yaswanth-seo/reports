Himalayan salt stone massage is a beautiful evolution of the traditional hot stone treatment. Instead of using smooth basalt (volcanic) stones, this practice uses hand-carved stones made from 100% pure Himalayan pink salt.
Because these stones contain 84 naturally occurring minerals and elements, they offer a bit more "oomph" than your standard river rock.
## Why Use Himalayan Salt Stones?
While basalt stones are great for heat retention, salt stones add a chemical and energetic layer to the massage:
* **Light Exfoliation:** The texture of the stone gently removes dead skin cells, leaving the skin smoother than a standard massage would. * **Mineral Absorption:** As the stones warm up, they release minerals (like magnesium, potassium, and calcium) that can be absorbed topically. * **Ionic Balance:** Proponents suggest that when heated, the salt helps emit negative ions, which can help reduce the "electro-smog" caused by our constant proximity to devices. * **Deep Relaxation:** The heat allows the therapist to get into the muscle tissue deeper and faster without needing heavy, painful pressure.
## How the Treatment Works
1. **Preparation:** The stones are warmed in a specialized professional heater (usually a dry heat source, as water will eventually dissolve the salt). 2. **The Technique:** A therapist typically applies a light layer of oil to your skin first. They then use the stones as an extension of their hands, using long, flowing strokes (Effleurage) to melt away tension. 3. **Targeted Work:** The stones come in various shapes—rounds for palms, teardrops for trigger points, and rectangles for the back—to hit specific knots.
