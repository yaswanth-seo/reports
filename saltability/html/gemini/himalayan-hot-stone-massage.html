Himalayan hot stone massage is a specialized therapy that swaps traditional basalt (volcanic) stones for hand-carved stones made of **pink Himalayan salt**.
It’s often described as a "three-in-one" treatment because it combines the thermal benefits of a hot stone massage, the chemical benefits of salt minerals, and a light physical exfoliation.
### Key Benefits
* **Mineral Infusion:** Himalayan salt contains 84 trace minerals (including magnesium, calcium, and potassium). As the stones heat up, these minerals are thought to be absorbed by the skin. * **Natural Exfoliation:** The texture of the salt stones gently sloughs off dead skin cells, leaving the skin smoother than standard smooth river stones. * **Deep Relaxation:** The heat helps penetrate muscle tissue, allowing the therapist to address tension without needing to apply heavy, painful pressure. * **Negative Ions:** Proponents suggest that heated salt stones release negative ions, which may help reduce the "electro-smog" caused by daily device use and boost your mood.
### What to Expect During a Session
1. **Preparation:** The stones are warmed in a specialized heater (usually a dry heat, as water would dissolve the salt). 2. **Application:** The therapist uses the stones as an extension of their hands, gliding them over the body using Swedish massage techniques. 3. **Placement:** Stones may be left on specific "trigger points" or energy centers to allow heat to radiate deep into the muscle.
