## Why Add Himalayan Salt?
Beyond the warm, amber glow that looks incredible in a dim room, salt enthusiasts point to several key benefits:
* **Respiratory Support:** Inhaling microscopic salt particles can help clear mucus and reduce inflammation in the airways (great for allergy sufferers).[pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/34726628/) * **Ionization:** When heated, salt is believed to release negative ions, which may help counteract "electronic smog" and improve mood. * **Skin Health:** Salt is naturally antibacterial and antimicrobial, which can help detoxify pores as you sweat. * **Atmosphere:** The soft, pink light is naturally calming and helps lower cortisol levels.[pharmeasy.in](https://pharmeasy.in/story/amazing-health-benefits-of-pink-himalayan-salt#:~:text=Himalayan%20pink%20salt%2C%20rich%20in,enhance%20relaxation%20for%20better%20sleep.)

## ⚠️ The "Salt & Metal" Warning
This is the most important part: **Salt is corrosive.** If you have a traditional electric heater, salt can significantly shorten its lifespan if it comes into direct contact with the heating elements or the metal casing.[www.salussaunas.com](https://www.salussaunas.com/blogs/blog/heater-maintenance-best-practices)
* **Do not** put salt directly on the sauna stones. * **Do** use a dedicated stainless steel or ceramic holder if placing salt near the heat source. * **Check your warranty:** Some sauna heater manufacturers void the warranty if salt is used improperly.
## Pro-Tips for the Best Experience
1. **Keep it Dry:** Salt absorbs moisture (it’s hygroscopic).[www.sigmaaldrich.com](https://www.sigmaaldrich.com/US/en/search/hygroscopic-salt?focus=sitecontent&page=1&perpage=30&sort=relevance&term=hygroscopic%20salt&type=site_content) If your sauna gets too humid (like a steam room), the salt will start to "weep" or melt, creating a messy brine.[www.salussaunas.com](https://www.salussaunas.com/blogs/blog/himalayan-salt-bricks-for-sauna#:~:text=The%20high%20moisture%20content%20will,rather%20than%20a%20wellness%20wall.) 2. **Backlighting:** If you install a salt wall, use **LED strips** behind the bricks.[esaunashop.com](https://esaunashop.com/led-lighting-for-himalayan-salt-bricks-white) The heat from the sauna combined with the light creates that iconic "salt cave" glow. 3. **Clean Up:** If you notice salt residue on wooden benches, wipe it down with a damp cloth after the sauna has cooled to prevent the wood from drying out or staining.
