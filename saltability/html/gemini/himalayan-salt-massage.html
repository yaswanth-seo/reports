It’s not just a fancy pink glow for your room; Himalayan salt massage is a unique therapeutic treatment that levels up a standard Swedish massage by adding mineral-rich heat and gentle exfoliation.
Think of it as a **three-in-one treatment**: heat therapy, mineral infusion, and skin smoothing.
## How It Works
Instead of just using their hands, the therapist uses warm, hand-carved **Himalayan salt stones**. These stones are heated to a comfortable temperature (usually around 49°C to 54°C) and applied to the body with Swedish massage strokes.
### The Benefits
* **Deep Relaxation:** The heat from the stones penetrates deep into the muscle tissue, melting away tension faster than a traditional massage. * **Mineral Absorption:** Himalayan salt contains 84 trace minerals (like magnesium, potassium, and calcium). While the "detox" claims are sometimes exaggerated, the minerals are excellent for skin health. * **Exfoliation:** The texture of the salt gently sloughs off dead skin cells, leaving your skin feeling incredibly soft. * **Negative Ions:** Proponents suggest that heated salt releases negative ions, which may help reduce stress and improve mood—similar to the feeling of being near a waterfall or the ocean.
