GA vs Stripe Gap
Stripe consistently shows more paid subs than GA tracks on the thank-you page. Over the overlap period, Stripe recorded 447 paid vs 387 GA users — a ~15% tracking gap. Could be ad blockers, GA script delays, or redirect timing.
Paid Ads Driving 26%
Google CPC is the second biggest channel at 26.1% of thank-you views. Combined with organic (20%), Google overall accounts for ~46% of converting traffic — nearly matching direct.
Failure Spikes
Jan 16 and Jan 27 had the highest failure counts during this period (22 and 18 respectively). These spikes correlate with high GA activity days, suggesting real demand that wasn't captured.
